# TYPERIFT 🫧🏎️

**Drift into Focus.**

Typerift is a premium, minimalist typing game designed for speed and style. It features a high-fidelity glassmorphic UI, an "intelligent" character-based WPM tracking system, and interactive background elements.

## Features
- **Intelligent WPM**: Tracks speed based on correct characters (industry standard), not just full words.
- **Glassmorphic UI**: Beautiful, soft-gradient design with a premium circular progress timer.
- **Interactive Background**: Glass bubbles that dodge your cursor as you prepare for your run.
- **High Fidelity Audio**: Custom audio engine for correct/wrong keys and game transitions.
- **Social Sharing**: Generate and download a premium score card to share on X, WhatsApp, or Telegram.

## How to Play
1. Select your time duration (20s, 30s, or 60s).
2. Click **IGNITE DRIFT**.
3. Start typing! Backspace is disabled for maximum focus.
4. Download your Drift Card and challenge your friends!
